Our Collection of Games

Bullet Maze Mayham

You are chosen to fight in a futuristic form of entertainment full of violence. The longer you survive within the arena, the stronger and more expensing your gear will become to survive longer. In this DOOM style game; you will have to travel through hallways, clear out rooms full of enemies and solve puzzles to enter new rooms.

Undead Crypt Enicma

You wake up and find yourself trapped in a cemetery, locked away in a crypt. This crypt is not normal crypt however, the residents of the crypt have arisen from the dead. The inside of the crypt is long and winding. If you explore the tunnels and rooms, you may be able to find keys to unlock and escape your prison. Be careful not to be consumed by the wandering residence inside the crypt.

Galactic Zombie Frontier

You and your crew voyage to a colony that lost contact with home base. Once your spacecraft reaches the colony planet, Everyone realizes the people on the planet have been turned into undead. You contact the Head Captain of the situation. Higher ups have told you to collect the IDs of high-ranking members on the planet. Try to collect the IDs while avoiding becoming a part of the lost colony.
